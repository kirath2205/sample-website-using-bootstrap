What is Hack NTU?

HackNTU is NTU's premier hackathon, jointly organised by SearchElect and NTU Students' Union. This is the inaugural grand iteration of HackNTU.

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

Students of all levels in any educational institution in Singapore & South-East Asia can sign up. This is a great opportunity for those aiming to learn more or pursue a career in information technology.

IS THERE A REGISTRATION FEE?

No! Admission is free. Just register online and wait for confirmation. We'll provide food, caffeine, Wi-Fi and a place for you to hack for the entire duration of the event.
